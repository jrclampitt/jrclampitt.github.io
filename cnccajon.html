Designed in Fusion 360 and milled using a Shopbot with a quarter inch bit, this cajon (or Peruvian box drum) packs both deep bass tones and high snare sounds when the face is struck. The 3/4 inch plywood is finished using a beeswax and orange oil wood polish and conditioner.
